Welcome To Study Point

'STUDY POINT - TUITION CENTRE' is the dream of a young talented and ambitious teacher Mrs. Sandhya saju, who believes that "A good teacher can inspire hope, ignite the knowledge and instill a love of learning;- Brad Henry".She always spread her positive vibes to her students. Having dedicated her life to the spreading of knowledge, she officially founded 'STUDY POINT-TUITION CENTRE' at Puzhavathu in Chaganacherry. Rendering's whole-hearted support is her friend Vidhya Rani J, who extends her support as the center administrator.
